Join us at the YKK London Showroom as we celebrate a decade of inspiring creativity with two incredible designers Bianca Saunders and Saul Nash.

From early student visits to shaping the future of fashion, it's a journey we're proud to witness. Bianca Saunders and Saul Nash first came to the YKK London Showroom as students, looking for the right fastenings to bring their ideas to life. Today they're leading globally recognised fashion brands and we're honoured to have supported their journey.

Expect drinks, music, and a window display honouring their creative journeys with us.

Bianca, who began visiting the showroom during her studies at Kingston University and later at the Royal College of Art, now runs her namesake label blending British tailoring with cultural storytelling. In 2024, she was honoured with the Fashion Museum's Dress of the Year.

Saul, who first explored our deadstock coil zippers, turned early experimentation into a signature design approach. His boundary-pushing work has since earned the Queen Elizabeth II Award for British Design, the International Woolmark Prize, and a finalist spot for the LVMH Prize.

Join us as we celebrate 10 years of creative connection with these two incredible designers.
