Exploring "Unfixed": A Journey into Fluidity and Hybridity Guided by Queer Ecology

“Unfixed” explores the fluidity of existence, celebrating hybridity and the endless possibilities of coexistence. Through prototypes and behind-the-scenes insights, you'll witness our transformation of body, identity, materials, and movement.

Join us to explore a world beyond binaries, inspired by the multiverse and the interconnectedness of all species.
